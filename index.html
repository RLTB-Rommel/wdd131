Palawan Island

Explore the Philippines, an enchanting archipelago of 7100 islands situated in Southeast Asia. Renowned for its unparalleled biodiversity, the country is home to 25% of the world's plant and animal species, with an impressive 80% being endemic to its shores. Among its unique wildlife treasures are the diminutive Tarsier, the world's smallest primate, and the majestic Philippine eagle, known for its diet that occasionally includes monkeys. Nature enthusiasts and adventurers will find the Philippines a haven of exceptional natural wonders and a profound biodiversity hotspot.
