Rafting Site Plan

Overview

Purpose

My water rafting website is tailored to thrill-seeking individuals aged 18 to 45, who have a moderate to high disposable income and a passion for outdoor adventure. These enthusiasts are eager to break free from their daily routines and seek exhilarating experiences. When accessing the website, they expect comprehensive information about available rafting trips, including difficulty levels, durations, safety measures, and pricing details. The website aims to meet their needs by serving as a centralized platform that not only showcases exciting rafting opportunities but also guides them through the planning process, ensuring safety and enjoyment. In terms of accessibility, my audience is likely to use various devices, with desktops and laptops being common during initial trip planning, and mobile devices becoming crucial when on the go or sharing information with friends. The user-friendly navigation menu and clear categorization enable them to quickly find family-friendly trips or access detailed safety measures for specific rafting adventures, providing a seamless and informative experience.

Audience

Scenario Questions:

1. "Can I find a rafting trip suitable for my skill level?"

Answer: Absolutely, our website provides clear difficulty ratings for each rafting trip, ensuring you can easily choose an adventure that matches your skill and comfort level.

2. "What safety measures are in place for the rafting trips?"

Answer: Safety is our priority. Detailed information on safety guidelines, provided equipment, and emergency procedures for each trip is readily available on the website.

3. "Are there family-friendly rafting options available?"

Answer: Yes, discover family-friendly adventures easily with our dedicated section or filter, offering trips with milder rapids and suitable options for beginners of all ages.
