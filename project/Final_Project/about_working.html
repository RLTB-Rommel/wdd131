About Us

Our company is for thrill-seeking individuals aged 18 to 45, who have a moderate to high disposable income and a passion for outdoor adventure. These enthusiasts are eager to break free from their daily routines and seek exhilarating experiences. When accessing the website, they expect comprehensive information about available rafting trips, including difficulty levels, durations, safety measures, and pricing details.

Embark on an unforgettable journey with Rapids Water Rafting Adventure, where the river's pulse meets the adventurer's heartbeat! Join Rapids and break free from the ordinary! Our trips are curated for those who seek the extraordinary — a perfect blend of adrenaline, nature, and camaraderie. History

In the heart of Cagayan de Oro, Philippines, a group of passionate adventurers came together with a shared dream to bring the exhilarating experience of white-water rafting to the world. In 2005, Rapids Water Rafting Company was born.
Founded by a team of outdoor enthusiasts who were captivated by the untamed beauty of the Cagayan River.
