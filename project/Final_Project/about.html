History

In Negros Occidental, Philippines, a consortium of visionary solar power firms came together with a bold vision to spearhead the adoption of solar technology across the province. Their journey began with the establishment of seven plants, boasting a total capacity of 341.5 megawatts (MW).

At the forefront stands Solar Tech Solutions in Cadiz City, Southeast Asia's largest, generating 132.5 MW and illuminating the path towards sustainable energy leadership in the region.

This pioneering initiative not only revolutionized the local energy landscape but also set a precedent for renewable energy development nationwide. By harnessing the abundant sunlight of Negros Occidental, these firms have not only reduced carbon footprints but also empowered communities through job creation and economic growth.

Founded by a team of solar energy enthusiasts who were captivated by the untapped potential of solar power in Negros Occidental, Solar Tech Solutions has been at the forefront of renewable energy innovation in the region.

Their pioneering efforts have not only transformed the local energy landscape but have also paved the way for sustainable development initiatives across the province. With a steadfast commitment to environmental stewardship and technological innovation.
